Brokerage

Real estate is one of the largest budget expenses of any business. For that reason, choosing the location of its office, retail, or industrial facility is one of the most important decisions a company can make. F.H. French has the expertise and market knowledge to advise businesses on how to select the location that best meets their financial, operational, and other business objectives.

Our brokerage service focuses on advising clients with real estate decisions such as site selection, property disposition, portfolio management, project management, and lease negotiation. Additionally, our tenant representation services concentrates on advising clients with real estate decisions including site selection, property disposition, portfolio management, project, management, and lease negotiation.

F.H. French offers a unique and flexible step-by-step process that focuses on the needs of each company by providing the following services: analyzing and defining existing space requirements; performing lease audits; survey and investigation of available properties; property tours and evaluation; lease negotiation, analysis, and execution; and construction management of tenant improvements.

At F.H. French, we don’t think in terms of space for lease or sale, we think in terms of solutions. Our team is knowledgeable, highly motivated and dedicated to finding the right real estate solutions to help grow your business.

If your business is located in Rhode Island or Southern New England, you would be hard-pressed to find anyone that knows the market better than F.H. French.

With every project we undertake, whether office, medical, industrial, land or flex space, we deliver the highest level of service to our clients. We begin by putting the client’s needs first and proceed to take a hands-on approach in identifying solutions that meet those needs. As a full-service commercial real estate firm, we have a vast amount of resources that can be utilized on any given project, including the counsel of our in-house experts in the areas of market research, finance, and construction management. Our ultimate goal is making recommendations that enable our clients to make informed real estate decisions.

Our philosophy is simple: Adding value while achieving trust and loyalty through providing the best service and empowering our clients in the ever-changing real estate climate. Long-term relationships are F.H. French’s hallmark.
